# VymoaGaurd PHM

VymoaGaurd PHM is a space mission-assurance prototype for conjunction-risk classification, telemetry anomaly detection, and deterministic operator triage.

## Evidence boundary

The prototype must show its evidence, uncertainty, provenance, and limitations. It is not flight-certified software and must not generate autonomous maneuver commands.
